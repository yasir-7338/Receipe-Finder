About Recipe Finder and Meal Planner

Welcome to Recipe Finder and Meal Planner — your ultimate companion in discovering delicious recipes and organizing your weekly meal plans with effortlessness. Our platform helps you to explore a variety of recipes based on ingredients you already have. You can also create and download custom weekly meal plans to keep your meals organized and healthy.

Features:

  • Discover recipes based on ingredients name or recipe name.
  • Filter recipes by dietary preferences (Vegetarian, Non-Vegetarian).
  • Organize your weekly meals.
  • Download your weekly meal plan as a PDF.
